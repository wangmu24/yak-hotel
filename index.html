Himalayan Warmth
Above the Clouds

Nestled in the heart of Namche Bazar at 3,440 metres, Yak Hotel & Restaurant has welcomed trekkers, mountaineers, and travellers from around the world for years. Our hotel stands as a warm refuge amid the breathtaking grandeur of the Khumbu Valley.

Every room frames views of the towering peaks — Ama Dablam, Thamserku, and on clear days, the distant silhouette of Everest herself. Our restaurant serves hearty Nepali and continental cuisine, fueling adventures in the world's highest mountains.

"The mountains are calling, and you must go — but first, rest well at Yak Hotel."

Warm Accommodations

From cozy Standard rooms to spacious Deluxe suites, each room is thoughtfully appointed with mountain views, warm bedding, and the comforts needed after a long day on the trails.

Authentic Cuisine

Our restaurant serves traditional Nepali dishes — dal bhat, momos, sherpa stew — alongside international favourites. Every meal is made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients.

Prime Location

Situated in the heart of Namche Bazar, we are steps from the famous Saturday market, the Edmund Hillary Museum, and all major trekking routes towards Everest Base Camp.
