Reaching Namche Bazar

Namche Bazar is the gateway to the Khumbu region. Here's how to reach us on your Himalayan journey.

✈ By Air — Lukla

Fly from Kathmandu to Tenzing-Hillary Airport, Lukla (LUA) — one of the world's most thrilling runways. Daily flights operate with mountain airlines. Flight duration is approx. 35 minutes.

🥾 Trekking from Lukla

From Lukla, trek through the beautiful Dudh Kosi valley. Pass through Phakding and Monjo before the famous climb to Namche Bazar. The trek typically takes 2 days at a comfortable pace.

🚁 Helicopter Charter

For groups or those with time constraints, helicopter charters to Namche Bazar are available from Kathmandu. We can help arrange transfers — just ask our team when you contact us.
